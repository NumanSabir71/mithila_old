This saree was painted by Savita Devi, a master artist from Ranti village, Madhubani. She learned the Kohbar style from her mother at age 12 and has been painting for over 25 years.

The Lotus & Peacock motif on this piece takes approximately 18 hours of continuous painting across 3 days. Each brushstroke is made with handcrafted bamboo nibs and natural pigments derived from plants, minerals, and earth.

Your purchase directly supports Savita and the 200+ women artisans in our cooperative — ensuring fair wages and preserving this 2,500-year-old living art tradition.
