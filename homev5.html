Born in Madhubani,
Worn Worldwide

For over 2,500 years, the women of Mithila have painted walls and sacred surfaces with stories of gods, nature, and love — carried onto silk and cotton worn worldwide.

  • UNESCO-recognized Intangible Cultural Heritage
