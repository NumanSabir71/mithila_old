Our Heritage

Born in Bihar,
Worn Worldwide

In the villages of Madhubani, women have painted the walls of their homes with vivid, sacred imagery for over 2,500 years. Mithila Design was founded to bring these ancient brushstrokes onto the finest fabrics.

We work directly with over 200 master artisans — primarily women — ensuring fair wages, creative ownership, and a living craft tradition passed from mother to daughter.

The Craft

How Every Piece Comes to Life

01
🌿
Natural Fabrics

We source only the finest handwoven cotton, silk, and chanderi from certified sustainable cooperatives across Bihar.

02
🎨
Artisan's Hand

Each motif — fish, lotus, sun, peacock — is sketched freehand using traditional bamboo pens and natural pigments.

03
✨
Quality Finishing

Every garment undergoes rigorous quality checks and natural washing to ensure the art lasts a lifetime on your fabric.

04
📦
Conscious Delivery

Packaged in recycled cloth bags with a handwritten note from the artisan — art you can trace back to its creator.
